Frequently Asked Questions

Our team of designers tirelessly publishes designs for you to work with. Looking for industrial with boho accents or minimalist with a pop of color? Our collections cover every style. But it doesn't end there. Start with an example design and remix it to create a multitude of looks.

Can I add furniture I already own to the design?

Yes! You can use our product search tools to recreate your room with products you already own. Dabble will immediately start recommending new items you can pair with things you own. We're building some exciting new features for this very problem and we'll be announcing more soon!

Is this for living rooms only?

We're starting off with living rooms to keep things simple. But we're working on adding more rooms as we get requests from users. For example, if you're looking to design a work-from-home setup, let us know!

How do you ensure products are in stock?

Our servers constantly check every vendor's product page to ensure that items are in stock and available to ship to your location. Unlike Pinterest, you'll never get to a dead-end product on Dabble.

How much will Dabble cost?

Dabble is free to use. You can explore and create as many designs as you like. We're also partnering with furniture vendors to get you exclusive deals and savings on products in our collections.

Let's make designing your home fun again.

We believe that anyone can have a beautifully designed space on any budget. Now let's get started designing your space.
